Message from the Vice Chancellor

I extend a warm welcome to all students joining Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University. The hostel life at SMVDU is designed to provide you with a nurturing environment that complements your academic journey. Our hostels are not just places of residence; they are communities where lifelong friendships are forged, leadership skills are developed, and personal growth is encouraged.

At SMVDU, we believe that a well-rounded education extends beyond the classroom. Our hostel facilities are equipped with modern amenities to ensure your comfort and well-being. The Warden's Council, comprising dedicated faculty members, works tirelessly to maintain a harmonious and disciplined atmosphere that fosters both personal and academic excellence.

I emphasize the importance of discipline, mutual respect, and adherence to hostel rules and regulations. These guidelines are essential for creating a safe and conducive living environment for all residents. Every student is expected to contribute positively to the hostel community and uphold the values of our university.

Ragging is strictly prohibited at SMVDU. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of ragging, whether on campus or off-campus. If you encounter any such incident, please report it immediately to your warden, any faculty member, or the university administration. Your safety and well-being are our top priorities.

I encourage all students to actively participate in hostel activities, maintain regular communication with their wardens, and make the most of their time at SMVDU. The hostel experience is an integral part of your university journey, and I hope it will be memorable and enriching.

I wish you all a successful and fulfilling academic journey at Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University. May your stay in our hostels be comfortable, productive, and contribute significantly to your overall development.

Thanking you and with regards,

Dr. Pragati Kumar
Vice Chancellor
Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University
