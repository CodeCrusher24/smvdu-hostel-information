Hostel Facilities

Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University provides comfortable and well-equipped hostel facilities for both boys and girls. The hostels are designed to create a home-like environment that supports academic excellence and personal growth.

SMVDU currently has 5 boys hostels (Trikuta, Kailash, Vindhyachal, Nilgiri, and Basohli) and 3 girls hostels (Shivalik A, Shivalik B, and Vaishnavi) that accommodate students from various programs.

The total capacity of hostels at SMVDU is approximately 800 for boys and 400 for girls.

All hostels are provided with the following facilities:

  • Well-furnished rooms with essential furniture
  • Solar water geysers for hot water supply
  • T.V. with cable connection in common rooms
  • Washing Machines
  • Indoor games facilities (Table Tennis, Carom & Chess)
  • Badminton Court
  • Gymnasium & Reading Room
  • High-speed LAN/Wi-Fi connectivity
  • 24/7 Security and CCTV surveillance
  • Mess facilities for nutritious meals
