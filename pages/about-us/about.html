About

Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University has 5 Boys and 3 Girls hostels in the campus that can accommodate around 1200 students. These hostels are:

Boys' Hostels

  1. Trikuta Hostel
  2. Kailash Hostel
  3. Vindhyachal Hostel
  4. Nilgiri Hostel
  5. Basohli Hostel

Girls' Hostels

  1. Shivalik A Hostel
  2. Shivalik B Hostel
  3. Vaishnavi Hostel

Mess services are provided in selected hostels to cater to the nutritional requirements of all students residing within the campus. The mess facilities ensure hygienic and nutritious meals for all residents.

Every hostel has its own common room, gymnasium, and reading room. Additionally, every hostel subscribes to the latest magazines and newspapers for the residents to stay updated with current affairs.

The hostels are connected to the campus via the campus-wide Wi-Fi network and LAN which enables the residents to browse the internet and access the online library resources for their academic and research-related work.
