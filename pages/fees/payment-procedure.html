Payment Procedure for Hostel & Mess Fee

Students can pay their Hostel & Mess fees online during the hostel allotment process using the official SMVDU Hostel payment links.

Steps to pay Hostel Fee:

  • Visit the SMVDU Hostel website and open the Hostel Allotment/Payment link.
  • Enter your Application/Enrollment number and verify your allotment details.
  • Proceed to payment and complete using NetBanking/Debit Card/UPI.
  • Download the receipt for your records.

Steps to pay Mess Fee:

  • Open the Mess Fee payment link on the hostel website.
  • Select your hostel: Trikuta, Kailash, Vindhyachal, Nilgiri, Basohli (boys) or Shivalik A, Shivalik B, Vaishnavi (girls).
  • Enter your details (Name, Enrollment, Room No.) and the payable amount.
  • Complete payment and save the receipt.

For any payment-related issues, write to hostels@smvdu.ac.in or contact the Hostel Office during working hours.
