Hostels - Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University

SMVDU provides residential facilities to students in a safe and supportive environment. Hostel accommodation is allotted based on eligibility and availability. The Hon'ble Vice Chancellor, Dr. Pragati Kumar, and the Dean of Students oversee the residential life to ensure student well‑being and discipline.

Boys' Hostels

  1. Trikuta Hostel
  2. Kailash Hostel
  3. Vindhyachal Hostel
  4. Nilgiri Hostel
  5. Basohli Hostel

Girls' Hostels

  1. Shivalik A Hostel
  2. Shivalik B Hostel
  3. Vaishnavi Hostel

All hostels offer well‑furnished rooms, reading rooms, indoor games, Wi‑Fi/LAN connectivity, and 24x7 security. Common mess facilities provide hygienic, nutritious meals.

Hostels are connected to the campus network for seamless access to online academic resources and services.
